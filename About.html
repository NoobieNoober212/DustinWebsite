Education Requirnments

Prerequisites include a High school diploma or similar in addition to satisfactory MCAT(Medical College Admition Test) scores.

Bachelor's degree as well as an M.D or D.O

Program Length for education

First you'll need a High School diploma or similar which usually takes about 4 years to get. Next will be a bachelor's degree. Obtaining a bachelor's degree usually takes around 4 years. Once you acquire a bachelor's degree you will need to go into medical school. Graduating medical school usually takes another 4 years. After you will need about 3-7 years in residency and fellowships depending on the specialty.

Bachelor's Degree

To get a Bachelor's degree it usually takes about four years of full-time studying. During these four years of study time, you will complete about 120 semester credits or around 40 college courses. If your college uses a quarter system rather than a semester system, you'll have to acquire a minimum of 180 quarter credits to obtain an accredited Bachelor's degree. Accredited meaning "officially recognized or authorized."

You can learn to become to things when being a basic doctor people visit for check ups etc.

One is called an M.D. and the other is a D.O.

M.D.

AN M.D. is an allopathic physician. An M.D. is educated in prescribing medicine, performing surgery, and practice in specialty areas. However, they do not receive some training that a D.O. would receive.

D.O.

A D.O. is an Osteopathic physician. While DO's are fully licensed in diagnosing and treating illnesses and disorders as well as providing care, they also are educated in some special training that allopathic physicians do not receive.When studying at a school that teaches people to be a Osteopathic physician, you’ll learn about Osteopathic Manipulative Treatment, or OMT for short.MD’s do not typically receive this training. OMT uses your own hands to diagnose and treat an illness or injury. Osteopathic Medicine also emphasizes the integration of the entire body's systems, and many of the osteopathic schools place special emphasis on preventive medicine.
